Help build the Internet's Database of Sports Mistakes. Submissions go into a review queue before they appear publicly.

Notability Standards

A submission should meet at least one of these:

  • It influenced the outcome of a game, season, playoff series, trade, draft, or championship.
  • It received media coverage or became widely discussed by fans.
  • It involved a major coaching, referee, player, ownership, league, or front-office decision.
  • It has historical significance.
  • It has a credible source that supports what happened.

Safety Rules

  • No hate speech, threats, harassment, or slurs.
  • No pornography or unsafe links.
  • No fake accusations or unsupported claims.
  • No spam, gambling spam, or unrelated promotions.

Bad, unsafe, or duplicate submissions may be rejected without review.
